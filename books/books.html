Wrong Guy

Shots fired, people dead. Time to find the hero within.

A spies and espionage novella with a touch of the sci-fi and an everyman hero.

Duane Higgins wants to get back with his family, but a bullet changes everything. Caught in the middle between a sexy foreign agent who want his secrets and others who want him dead, he must fight to protect his family and save the world, all the while battling his own personal demon in the bottle.

A fast read, a short-term commitment, a wham-bam, thanks for reading, Stan.

Swords and Magic

In a world of Norse Mythology, destiny confronts reality.

Kari has her dream as does Erik, but an upcoming baby will change their lives. How they work it out in their world is relevant in ours except they have dragons.

Kari wants to be a warrior, to find valor following in her deceased father’s footsteps. Erik is stuck in the family business of boat building though he was meant for a life at court. Kari is pregnant, her dream is ending, and she’s scared, unable to conjure up a maternal instinct. She’s also afraid of dragons, something as a warrior she’s working to overcome, so when a serpentine fiery beast confronts her . . .

Destiny in Norse Mythology is a strong force but is one self-will can overcome.

Grab your copy today.

Bound by Blood

Family matters.

A new adult getting her life back on the road after hitting a few potholes in her youth, Julia has neither the time nor energy to waste on either romance or suspense, but she’s got both.

As the proud new owner of a cute bungalow in need of repairs, she’s looking forward to remodeling her life with a hammer and paintbrush. She’s also excited to be growing in her Christian faith. This is her time to come of age, but there’s trouble brewing from two fronts.

When her Meth addicted brother returns to crash on her floor, his problems come too. Gangs are after him. He’s a dead man walking, and he’s reaching out for her.

She can’t relax at work either because the new construction foreman presents a different challenge with his infectious smile. Julia tries to stay full-on -board with her get-my-life together mode, so she’s not willing to become this playboy’s latest quest

To her brother and her Lord, she’s Bound by Blood. But Rick stirs her heart differently, and that just isn’t in her plans for redemption.

Of course, neither are the bullets flying nor the car chases. Between her brother and Rick, getting her life in order has become a crazy mess.

This is a re-release of the 2014 edition with a few edits made.

Murderous Justice

They left him with one desire and nothing more to lose.

What starts as a simple revenge trope goes off-track when the guy gets conflicted about killing. Dang, I hate it when that happens. Revenge, the nature of justice, and learning to move on. Scott Springer writing as Clyde McCall explores his signature themes of family and the lone hero, his modern day American Cowboy.

Carl lives the simple life in the woods with his perfect family, a loving wife and two adoring kids, until evil destroys all he breathes for in a fit of bloody rage.

Read More

Everyone knows who did it, but none dare question the powerful.

The five stages of grief and loss are denial, anger, and . . . Carl is stalled firmly in anger. There will be no acceptance until blood spills. To Carl, the one stage of dealing with grief is vengeance.

Carl licked dirt, but soon he’ll taste blood.

The bible says turn the other cheek, but Carl says eye for an eye. Unafraid to die, he pursues his murderous justice.

This small-town thriller pays homage to the pulp tradition with its fast pace and plenty of action. But it is also a thoughtful novel on what it means to achieve justice move forward.

Read it today.
